Card flip Rules

The cards

There are pairs of different types of card faces to be matched. the rules are simple:

  • click on the card to see the face behind it and then click on another card to match the face.
  • there are 8 pairs of cards to be matched, in the fastest time posssible
  • player with the best time record will top the leader board

Hope you enjoy playing it as much as i enjoyed making it!
